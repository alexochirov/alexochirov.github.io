Военное применение

Military personnel deserve the most advanced, most durable technology products available. Durabook computers are built rugged and designed to with stand the daily challenges of military task forces. Durabooks come with sunlight readable display, wireless connectivity and security features and rugged features the military needs in order to complete their mission. Durabook is proud to serve our military forces.

Durabook computers are designed to withstand the intense heat and sand of the desert, and the bumps and jolts of an armored vehicle. The reliability and ruggedness capabilities make it the ideal choice for military personnel. No longer worry about roughing the edges of your computer, no longer worry about your computer dying due to vibration and shock. With significantly longer uptime, lower operating costs, and improved productivity, Durabook computers are ideal for military.

To meet the military force demand, we suggest T7Q and TA10

Обслуживание на местах

Not only the versatility and mobility but also the toughness of the Durabook computers meet the requirements of every aspect of the public safety mission. Durabook provides an ideal solution for public safety personnel. These computers are built to operate in work environments that normal laptops can’t survive. Resist the vibration and shock on the road and in the field and still keep connection with headquarters. Touch panels and sunlight readable displays allow users to easily work and access information anywhere. These superior wireless connection and the capability of operating in various outdoor environments further increase the productivity of officers and agents to achieve their missions.

To meet the public safety application, we suggest T7Q, U12Ci, SA14, TA10 and CA10.
